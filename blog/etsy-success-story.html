💡 From Stay-at-Home Mom to Etsy Star – Her $10K/Month Canva Side Hustle 💡

Meet Sarah, a 32-year-old stay-at-home mom from Ohio who turned her hobby into a digital goldmine. Just a year ago, she was googling "how to make money online from home" and stumbled across the world of Etsy printables.

With zero graphic design experience, Sarah started using Canva (yes, the free version!) to create simple planners, habit trackers, and kids' chore charts. Her first listing? A pastel morning routine chart – it didn’t blow up overnight, but it got traction. 💥

🚀 The Viral Boost

Three months in, one of her self-care planner bundles was picked up by a YouTuber reviewing digital products on Etsy. Sales skyrocketed! Sarah went from a few bucks a day to $400+ daily within a week. That video alone drove over 10,000 visitors to her shop!

🎯 Her Winning Strategy

  • SEO-Optimized Titles: She used tools like eRank to find keywords like "daily digital planner for moms." 📈
  • Niche Focus: Instead of trying everything, she focused on wellness planners and kids' products. 🎯
  • Consistent Uploads: She uploaded a new product every week for 6 months straight.

💸 Now She Makes $10,000/Month

Sarah’s store now brings in over $10K/month in passive income. She even started a second Etsy shop for wedding templates – all designed in Canva.

What’s her advice? “Just start. Even if it’s ugly. You’ll improve fast and your shop will grow.”
